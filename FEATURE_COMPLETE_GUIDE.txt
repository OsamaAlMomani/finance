   Used in: Cash Flow Timeline, Cumulative Savings

   Used in: All balance tracking

   Used in: Burn Rate & Runway, Danger Meter

4. Runway = Cash Ã· Burn Rate
   Used in: Burn Rate & Runway, Scenario Planner

   Used in: Danger & Safety Meter
